# Contract Law Review Context

## Review Criteria
- Governing law and jurisdiction
- Liability and indemnification clauses
- Termination and renewal terms
- Confidentiality and data protection
- Payment terms and penalties
- Force majeure provisions

## Risk Ratings
- Critical: Legal compliance violation, unlimited liability exposure
- High: Unfavorable jurisdiction, one-sided termination rights
- Medium: Ambiguous terms, missing standard clauses
- Low: Minor wording improvements, formatting issues
